Super Stephanie Salter has spent the past few months back in Indiana helping her father who is battling cancer. Committee Member Susan Anderson-Norby arranged to have a basket of fruit sent to Stephanie to let her know that her Super friends were thinking about her during this difficult time.

Stephanie recently sent this acknowledgment to The Spearhead:

This is so tardy, but please post many thanks to the Supers (most especially that generous and cunning Susan Anderson) for the crate of dee-licious fruit that arrived earlier this month in Indiana.

I am still here, helping to take care of my dad who has advanced lung cancer. And it looks as though I will be seeing more productions at Indiana University, Indianapolis and the Chicago Lyric this year than I will at SFO. (I'll be happy to provide some reviews!)

I miss you all and I miss our beloved city. Take care of the Opera House for me. My spirit will be with you all, especially during Tosca.

Toi, toi, toi,

Stephanie Salter
