Rehearsal Schedules

If you have questions please contact your PSC or Kristina Chang at 415-565-3259.

Important Notice!

These dates and times, with the exception of performances, are tentative staging rehearsals and may or may not include supers. Depending on the production, some supers may not be called to every rehearsal. Dress rehearsal and performance dates do not include Makeup/Dress call times which will be determined closer to the date.

This online schedule will not receive regular updates and is provided only to give an idea of the potential rehearsal/performance commitment required for each production. Additional rehearsal dates may be scheduled according to the needs of the production. Daily schedule changes and updates will be provided by your Production Super Captain via email as they become available.

ALL REHEARSALS DATES AND TIMES ARE SUBJECT TO CHANGE OR CANCELLATION ON SHORT NOTICE.
