Jacket Order

Always admired the gorgeous jackets the SFOpera in-crowd wears? If you're a Super or a lightwalker (and have $200), you can have one too! The price includes your name on the front, and for a few more bucks you can have "Supers" or "Lightwalker" or whatever you like embroidered on the back. Chorister Chris Corley needs a total of twelve orders before he can get them made. The order form is here,  and you can contact Chris by email for further information.
