Hi Gang:

Hope you are all well! It was fun to see the photos from the super party on the website. What a marvelous idea that has turned out to be!

Has SFO been treating you well? I meant to come up for opening weekend this year, but didn't get my act together. Actually, it's been crazy here — we've got a ton of shows and shorts in the works. I hope to make it up there for the summer festival and Merola. I'm ashamed to admit that I haven't seen an opera since leaving SFO. However, that will soon be rectified on Friday: the Alagnas are doing Bohème here at the LA Opera. You'll get a kick out of this: my friend Bill Williams (who coordinates the chorus and Supers for LA Opera) asked if I would be available should someone drop out of Aida — talk about coming full circle. The schedule is actually very manageable so I may do it. (Falstaff with Bryn Terfel and Romeo with Anna Netrebko are also tempting Super roles; Rosenkavalier is not — just SOOOOO long on a school night.)

Disney has been great — I love the people and the work is interesting. I've been dating someone (Scott) for 6 months now. The easiest and most normal relationship I've ever had. He's a publicist for F/X and works on the drama Nip/Tuck. (They were very happy this week to get a bunch of Golden Globe noms.)

Write back and let me know how you are doing, catch me up with gossip, etc. I spoke to Hally Albers yesterday, who is stage managing the Bohème. She's very excited to be coming back to SF Opera this fall.

Happy Holidays and Best Wishes to you all!

Todd
