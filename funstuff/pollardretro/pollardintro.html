Oliver!

The date has been set for his farewell bash at Buco di Beppo. The date is Monday, September 22nd and the time 6:30. Space is, of course, limited and Ulrica knows that you will all want to bid Oliver "Addio" as he heads back to the Midwest. So please, if you plan on attending, RSVP by September 19th to my Social Secretary, Andrew Korniej, at 415/550-0456 or via email at akorniejsupers@yahoo.com . Buco is located on Howard between Fourth and Fifth streets, in fashionable SOMA. Expect to pay $20-25 for as much good pasta as you can eat. See you there! Nancy Huie suggests we all bring snapshots of ‘Ollie' to roast him with.

We are pleased to look back at Olliver Pollard's illustrious career as a Supernumerary with San Francisco Opera. Proceed to the Gallery for a look at some more great pictures.
