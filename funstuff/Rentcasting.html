Would you like to do extra work on the movie RENT?

Beau Bonneau Casting is casting extras for RENT, the feature film based on the Broadway hit musical. RENT is being directed by Christopher Columbus,and stars Taye Diggs, Rosario Dawson and Jesse L. Martin. Filming begins later this month in the Bay Area and will last through June 2005! Non Union Extra work pays $68 for up to 8hrs plus overtime thereafter. There will more than likely be overtime as film shoots are long, often lasting 12 hours or more. *If selected as an Extra With Car, there is an additional $35 for your vehicle. These are standard film extra rates set by production, not by Beau Bonneau Casting.

We have an immediate need for the following types: ARTISTS, BOHEMIANS, MUSICIANS, STREET PEOPLE, JUNKIES, DRUG DEALERS, PIMPS & STREET WALKER TYPES plus CARS for extra work & the SF CASTING DATABASE HAS EXTENDED REGISTRATION HOURS!

Registration hours are Monday - Friday 1:00PM - 5:30PM, effective 3/4 - 3/31/05.

Background Registration Information for RENT only:
Registration is FREE if you simply drop off a snapshot at our office, you must include your first & last name with contact information on the back of the photo. We will only call you if you are selected. Your photos will not be returned and will be discarded after the film wraps.

Registration with the SF Casting Network:
NON-UNION TALENT AND TALENT WITHOUT AN AGENT:
If you are interested in registering with the SF Casting Network database to hear about future projects that may include television, print and commercials, there is a $25.00 registration fee good for two years. You will have access to your online file and you will receive e-mails regarding upcoming projects. There is no guarantee of work. Casting directors will only call you if you fit the type they are looking for.

If you are a member of the Screen Actors Guild or represented by a SAG franchised talent agency, registration with the SF casting Network database is free. You must have your SAG card with you as proof of membership or agency paperwork for proof of association

Beau Bonneau Casting highly encourages the following types to come in ASAP:

ARTIST, BOHEMIAN & MUSICIAN TYPES: Men & Women, all ethnicities, 18-35, in shape, with an edge or interesting look for various scenes set in New York City during the late 1980's. Think the Ramones, Clash, Velvet Underground; we're looking for retro, early underground, alternative-punk types, barely making the rent in the East Village. If you have a car that is from 1975-early 90's that is a plus (and you can earn a little extra $$$$).

STREET PEOPLE, JUNKIES, DRUG DEALERS, PIMPS AND STREET WALKER TYPES: Men and Women, ALL ethnicities, 21-50s with interesting, character looks for various scenes set in New York City during the late 1980's.

GENERAL EXTRAS WITH CARS: Men and Women, all types and ethnicities, 18 to 65 years. We may need YOU or JUST YOUR CAR. Your vehicle must be manufactured 1975-early 90s. Car must be in decent condition, run well, and quietly. You must have a valid driver's license, and a clean driving record. NO RED, NO WHITE, & NO CONVERTIBLES will be considered. (Again keep in mind the film is set for the late 80's. If you have a newer make/model of car it may not be appropriate.)

If you fit the description, come in to our office at your earliest convenience, Monday - Friday 1:00PM - 5:30PM and DROP OFF the following:
One or two recent color photo(s) with First & Last Name, 2 Contact Phone Numbers & an Email address on the back of each photo. NOTE: Your photos Will Not Be Returned! IF YOU HAVE AN APPROPRIATE CAR, please submit one photo of your car only.

Beau Bonneau Casting is located at 84 1st Street, San Francisco, CA 94105.
We are very close to Mission St. (Kitty-corner from the Transbay Bus Terminal & 1 block from Montgomery Bart Station). No appointment is necessary, however you must come in between the times given.

***If Beau Bonneau Casting feels you may be appropriate for a particular role, we may ask to take digital photos of you or possibly cast you on the spot (which is why we ask you to come in to our office!) and you will be in the SF Casting database free for the duration of the film (the film will shoot through the month of June).

Please note that attending registration, or dropping off photos does not guarantee employment. We are casting specific types and will contact you only if selected.
