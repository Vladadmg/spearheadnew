Fall Bake Sale Dates Announced

The new Super Committee has announced the dates of the bake sales for the fall season.

The bake sales are the major fundraising events which offset the cost of the Annual Super Party which will be held at the Golden Gate Yacht Club on Sunday, November 19.

The last bake sale of the season will be during the matinee performances of Carmen on Sunday, December 3.



Details of the bake sales will be announced a few weeks before each sale. Supers are asked to bring a food item to sell, and to meet friends and purchase the delicious food items created by their fellow Supers.

All bake sales are held in the employee canteen.
