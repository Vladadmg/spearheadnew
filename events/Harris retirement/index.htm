Joe Harris Receives Super Award

Joe Harris, dresser to the stars, received a special award from SFO Supers at the final performance of La Bohème on July 2 2004. Joe was honored for his professionalism and for the kindness and friendliness he has shown to all the Supers who have worked with him over the years.
