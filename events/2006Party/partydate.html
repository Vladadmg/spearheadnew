Rigoletto Bake Sale

The Super Committee needs your help to make the next bake sale, scheduled for Sunday, October 29 at the matinee performance of Rigoletto, a success. Proceeds from the sale produce the Annual Super Party and keep the ticket cost low.

PLEASE consider bringing a food item to help the cause. Of course, home-made is always great, but something from your favorite shop or bakery is great too. There was an abundance of sweets and a limited number of savories at the last sale, so please keep that in mind. Salads, quiche, pasta dishes, etc. are a great addition. Set up begins at noon, and the sale will start at 12:30 and run through the first intermission, or until the food runs out, whichever comes first. Can we count on you to be there????

The Annual Party is scheduled for the evening of Sunday, November 19 at the Golden Gate Yacht Club in San Francisco.

Stay tuned for information on the details including tickets cost. Please note that attendance will be limited to Supers and ONE guest each. A word to the wise--the party sold out very quickly last year and many people were disappointed that they were not able to attend so be sure to respond as soon as the details are announced.
