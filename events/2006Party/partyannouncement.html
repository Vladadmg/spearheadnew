Super Party News

The 2006 Super Party will again be held at the Golden Gate Yacht Club, this year on Sunday, November 19, from 7 to 10 pm.

Following last year’s successful soirée, Super Sharon Castle has agreed to host our group at the club once again.

Those who attended last year’s party are still remarking about the breathtaking views of the bay and the Golden Gate, the sumptuous meal, and the glorious singing provided by former Merola Program participants.

This year's festivies will include a sumptuous family-style dinner catered by Michael Goldstein Events, presentation of the “Hammy” and Super-of-the-Year awards, and some very special entertainment. Festive opera costumes are encouraged.

This event has been sold out.
