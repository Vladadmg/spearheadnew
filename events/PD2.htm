The new Disney movie The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement opened on Wednesday, August 11. You know, Julie Andrews, Anne Hathaway (not Mrs. Shakespeare, the other one), etc. Well, Super favorite Anna Netrebko has a cameo in which she plays herself -- an international opera star -- in the movie, performing the aria "Sempre libera," oh-so-coincidentally the title of her new album to be released this month.

And on the non-Disney front, you can read about her secret ambition to be a stripper in Liz Smith's column.
