Fidelio Cast Party

San Francisco Opera General Manager Pamela Rosenberg honored Production Super Captain Charlie Lichtman and all of the 2005 Supers for their magnificent contribution to the season's success. Ms Rosenberg made her remarks at the cast party following the opening night performance of Fidelio on November 9th 2005.
