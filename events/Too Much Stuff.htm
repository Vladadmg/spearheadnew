Fabulous Garage Sale

Donate– Volunteer– Buy

Volunteers are needed for the following:

  • to work at the garage sale in two hour shifts, starting at 9 am and to help break it down

  • to price items on Friday April 23rd

  • to pick up and deliver donated items during the week before the sale

If you can help with any of the above, please contact Andrew Korniej at 416/585-2839 or via email.

 

Donations: Please donate the following before or during the sale:

  • Saleable items in good condition

  • Books, CDs, videotapes, DVDs

  • Small to medium items of furniture

  • Kitchenware and tchotchkes

But please

  • No clothes, records, typewriters or broken appliances

 

There will be limited pickups by arrangement (San Francisco only) during the week before for those who are unable bring items to the sale themselves. To arrange a pick up please call Andrew at 415/585-2839 or email. To arrange an evening drop off call Charlie at 415/641-8410 or email.
