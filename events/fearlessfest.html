The 2nd annual Fearless Tales Genre Fest needs volunteers.

Film festival dates are March 29 through April 3, 2005.

Fearless Tales Genre Fest celebrates independent noir, fantasy, sci-fi fantasy and horror films. In its second year and has an even stronger lineup than last year. Among other festival highlights, acclaimed director John Landis will be honored as the Fearless Vision award winner. John Landis will be in attendance to not only receive his award, but also participate in a rare Q & A session before presenting his horror classic "An American Werewolf in London." It will be an exciting night, and that's just the beginning. For more information about Fearless Tales and the Genre Fest (festival information still being finalized, so check back often), please visit www.fearlesstales.com

There are two venues this year, the Victoria Theater and the Castro Theater, so we'll need even more volunteers.Fearless Tales needs volunteers for this undertaking. Volunteers needed beginning immediately until the end of the festival.


Here are some of the possible volunteer jobs:
- Posting flyers
- Airport pickups
- Driving filmmakers to and from hotels and venues
- Ushering
- Cleaning theater after films
Volunteers will be given tickets to festival films (details to be worked out at a later date).

TO VOLUNTEER: Please contact Carrie Murphy at carrie@fearlesstales.com or call (415) 595-1725
