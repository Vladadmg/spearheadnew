2006 Garage Sale

This year's garage sale was hosted by Charlie Lichtman at his house in San Francisco's (usually) sunny Noe Valley. Despite a very chilly fog, and numerous other activities in the neighborhood, the sale resulted in a fabulous $801 downpayment on the 2006 Super Party. Many thanks to the devoted A-list supers, donors and volunteers, who made this sale a great success. And an extra thank you to Charlie for disposing of an unsold item or two via Craig's List. Maybe next year will be a combination garage sale and eSale.
