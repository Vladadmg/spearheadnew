Carrie's Moving On Party
1-20 | 21-36

It was a packed dining room at Buca de Beppo on April 4th, 2006 as thirty-plus hungry supers officially relieved Carrie of her Super Coordinator duties and wished her well in the continuation of her job. It was a great excuse for supers to get together and enjoy wonderful food and good friends. It's too bad April wasn't there since she could have simply walked through the room once and brilliantly cast the entire fall season.
