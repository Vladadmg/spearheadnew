A "60 Minutes" segment on Anna Netrebko on CBS last night (Sunday, December 12) showed some clips from last season's La Bohème. Supers Andrew Korniej and Sergio Suhett were particularly visible! If you missed it, you will be able to order a video from the CBS store shortly.

In other Anna news, her DVD The Woman, The Voice has finally been relased in the U.S. In Europe, it has been #1, outselling Britney Spears. In her interview, she reveals that her first onstage role was as a Super, as the rear leg of a horse in Le Coq d'Or.
