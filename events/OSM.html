La Sonnambula at Old Saint Mary's! 4/14 to 4/17/2005


Enjoy an evening of great music at Old Saint Mary's. California Opera will present a performance of Bellini's La Sonnambula at OSM on April 14th and 16th at 7:00PM, and April 17th at 3:00PM. Our own Larry Severino will be performing as a member of the chorus in this semi-staged, semi-concert production.

Tickets are on sale at the Paulist Center Bookstore for $25 general admission and $20 for seniors & students, or call 415-288-3845 for more information.

San Francisco Opera Supernumeraries and their guests qualify for a special group rate of only $15! Just show your SFO Super ID at the door.

Proceeds support the restoration of the church. Don't miss out on a great performance!

Old Saint Mary's Cathedral is at 660 California Street in San Francisco.
