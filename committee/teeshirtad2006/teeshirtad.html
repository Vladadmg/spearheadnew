San Francisco Opera Supernumeraries Tee Shirt

Hot off the Presses, and making its debut at the September 17th Bake Sale, is a chic new variation on the classic SF Opera Supers tee shirt. Originally designed by our own Susan Anderson, it bears the Super motto “Non Solum Hastas Sed Etiam Spectaculum” (We not only carry the spears, but also the show) over a design of a laurel wreath and crossed spears.

The logo has been hand screen printed in Antique Gold onto a black, high quality, 100% cotton tee shirt at San Francisco’s Babylon Burning and is available for $17 each (plus $4 postage and packing). It comes in sizes M, L and XL. They will be on sale at all Super functions and Bake Sales this Season, and the proceeds will go towards the Annual Super Party.

To order by mail, please send a check for $21 for the first shirt (plus $19 for any additional shirts) payable to “The Super Committee” to:

The Super Committee
c/o Katie Heibein
725 Evergreen Street
Menlo Park, Ca. 94025

Please be sure to specify the quantity and size(s) and include your shipping address.
