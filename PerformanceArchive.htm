Supers are invited use theOpera's long-awaited Performance Archive database, an amazing repository of information from the 1923 season to date, with complete casts!

A few things Supers will want to know:

  • our names are recorded in the "record" column, not the "cast"
  • we are often listed in the "Cast Page")
  • there is no Super data prior to 1970
  • from 1970 to 1992 all Supers in any of that season's productions are listed in all the programs. Usually.
  • from 1993 to date, all is well!
  • Don't forget to click on the icons for pictures from the production!
