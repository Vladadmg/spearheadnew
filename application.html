The Application Process

If you are interested in being a Supernumerary at the San Francisco Opera we ask you to follow these procedures:

Please review the Super Handbook posted on this website. There are a lot of DOs and DON’Ts attached to supering and it isn’t for everyone. It can be extremely time consuming and you will be expected to commit fully to any production for which you might be considered, including all rehearsal calls (including those which fall during work days) and, especially, each and every performance and dress rehearsal.

Print out and fill in the attached form with your personal information. This should be returned, with a recent photograph, to:

Super Coordinator
San Francisco Opera
War Memorial Opera House
301 Van Ness Avenue
San Francisco, CA 94102

You will be cast as needed. The Production Staff reviews the files of our Supers, and often selection is based primarily on size, for costuming. Every few years the Opera will mount a production that needs a lot of Supers (Aida, War and Peace, etc.) and at those times they will need more people.

Keep checking the Auditions page on this website where we will announce any specific calls for Supers.

The Opera will call you if you are under consideration for a role.

If you have any questions about supering or the application process please contact us through this website. We will be happy to help.
