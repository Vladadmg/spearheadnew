Welcome to Spearheadnews.com

...and to the Twenty-First Century. Although the print edition of "The Spearhead" has long been around, and will continue to exist in a limited format, the great majority of our Supers have access to the Internet, and this is the most effective (and fun) way to present features, photos, and timely information. Thanks to the fine designing skills of our webmaster, Mike Harvey, and some creative input from Andrew Korniej and Lynn Meinhardt, the articles submitted by our authors and columnists and the many fine photographs can be seen to best advantage.

All Supers are welcome, nay encouraged, to participate by sending digitized photos to Andrew. Small items and longer articles or opera reviews and general suggestions should be sent to me. Comments on website design or functionality should be sent to Mike. Gossip should be sent to Ulrica.

Supers, this is your site. Please give us your feedback. Happy browsing!
