Hello, Natalie!

Meet the new Super Coordinator, Natalie Dobranski . Natalie started temping at the SF Opera several months ago, covering the reception desk and doing various projects for the general director's office. In May, Natalie accepted the position of Scheduling Assistant (to Gretchen Mueller) in the Production department.
    
Having grown up in San Francisco, Natalie also studied theater for several years, acting in several performances, recording voiceovers, and attending classes at ACT. She graduated from Fordham University with a degree in business, then traveled through Australia and New Zealand for about a year, there discovering a love for scuba diving, bungee jumping, and sky diving. She said of becoming our Coordinator that she is “thrilled to take on this new and exciting challenge”; we trust riding herd on Supers will not be quite the adventure that jumping out of an airplane is, but we feel confident that sort of experience will come in handy when dealing with the free-fall that is the Opera in season.

Email her at ndobranski@SFOpera.com.
