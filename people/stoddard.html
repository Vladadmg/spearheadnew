Message from Don Stoddard

Hi to all the supers,

As some of you my have heard i was in motorcycle accident on May 7th. After four weeks and six operations I lost the fight to save my left foot.

After the initial disappointment, sadness and fear I find myself faced with a somewhat unique outlook on life. That is,everything is going to be alright maybe not so unique after all but I do feel it.

I've been walking on my temp leg for a week or so with the help of a cane. I'll have a new leg and foot by the end of the week. I'll be taking dance lessons within weeks and learn how to move again. Then I'll be ready to super .

This is just a new part I have to play and I'll give it my best.

Looking forward to seeing everyone this fall.

Peace,
Don
