Louis Schilling - Actor

Stage acting is now long-time Super Louis Schilling's biggest passion and, since the Super outing a few years ago to see him in Noel Coward's Present Laughter, he has performed in Enchanted April, Hedda Gabler, Grand Hotel-The Musical, The Golden Child and Man of La Mancha. Not to forget that he still gives his yearly appearance as Madame Du Cirque in San Francisco Ballet's Nutcracker, doing every one of about thirty performances.

Right now Louis is playing the lead in A Thousand Clowns by Herb Gardner, which opens this Thursday, January 24, at Hillbarn Theatre in Foster City. (Some might remember the movie version of the play with Jason Robards.)

The Examiner ran an article about the show a few days ago.

Tickets can be ordered online through the Hillbarn webpage.
