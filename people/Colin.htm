RIP Colin Graham

Stage director, librettist and Opera Theatre of St. Louis artistic director Colin Graham, 75, died Friday of heart failure. His SFO productions included:

1971 A Midsummer Night's Dream

1994 The Dangerous Liaisons

1996-1997 Hamlet

1997-1998 Pelléas et Mélisande

1998-1999 A Streetcar Named Desire

1999-2000 La Favorite

2000-2001 The Ballad of Baby Doe
