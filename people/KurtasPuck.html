Kurt Krikorian is Puck in A Midsummer Night's Dream

Festival Opera of Walnut Creek announced that San Francisco Opera Super Kurt Krikorian has been cast as Puck in Benjamin Britten's A Midsummer Night's Dream. The production will be directed and conducted by Michael Morgan with performances on August 9, 12, 15, and 17 2008 at the Lesher Center for the Arts in Walnut Creek.

Kurt was last seen on the SF Opera stage as the flying knight in Tristan und Isolde.
