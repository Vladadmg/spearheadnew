SAN FRANCISCO OPERA

 

Supernumerary Committee

 

November 17, 2003

 

Minutes of the Meeting

 

 

The following members of the Supernumerary Committee (the “Committee”) of the San Francisco Opera (the “Opera”) were present for all or parts of the meeting:

 

            Susan Anderson

            Irene Bechtel

            Mike Harvey

            Andrew Korniej

            Paul Newman

            Priscilla Lore

            Mimi Timberlake

            Sally Warren

 

            Also present were Carrie Murphy, who served as Chairman of the meeting, and Charlie Lichtman.  Priscilla Lore served as Secretary.

 

Carrie called the meeting to order at 6:30 pm. Minutes of the October 27, 2003, meetings were read and approved. The Financial Report was accepted.

 

Financial Report

 

Income was $1265 from receipts for the upcoming Super Party, $10 from Milko Encinas to place an ad in the Spearhead, and  $816 from the Bake Sale; expenses were more extensive ($818.50) than usual because of the Super Party: $30 to Robert Ericsson for Albert and Karen Goodwyn’s refund for the party, $250 to Mimi for the caterer’s deposit, $500 to Fort Mason as the remainder of the cost for the room and equipment, $38.50 to Charlie for opening night candy. Our balance is -$1,210 as of November 17, 2003.

Paul noted that opening night candies are expensive and that 20 percent of Bake Sale receipts end up going to candy. He followed this with an update of the guest count and money received for the Super Party.

 

Old Business

 

            Super Party: Mimi brought up the need to get our final guest count to the caterer; the Committee settled on 125. Mimi also reported on the menu and offered to pick up desserts at CostCo. Then followed a discussion of last year’s party that resulted in determining what supplies we needed this year. Susan offered to pick up coffee at Peet’s again. Andrew and Mimi offered to purchase the wine and other supplies.

           

Andrew reported on developments in the arrangements with Fort Mason re open flames and alcohol permits.

New Business

 

Super Party: The order of the program for the party was discussed followed by an extended discussion about the Hammy awards and who has earned one (N.B.: Paul invented the Hammy!).

 

Carrie left the meeting at 7:00. Irene left at 7:15. Departures were in no way connected with the discussion of the Hammies.

 

The meeting was adjourned at 7:50.

 

The next meeting will be January 7, 2004, at 6:30 pm in the 4th floor conference room of the Opera House. Priscilla will act as chair and Andrew will be secretary.
