SAN FRANCISCO OPERA

 

Supernumerary Committee

 

January 31, 2005

 

Minutes of the Meeting

 

 

The following members of the Supernumerary Committee (the “Committee”) of the San Francisco Opera (the “Opera”) were present for all or parts of the meeting:

 

            Susan Anderson

Irene Bechtel

Mike Harvey

            Priscilla Lore

            Paul Szczesiul

            Kimberly Thompson

            Rob Wonder

 

            Committee member Sally Warren was absent due to illness; Carrie Murphy was absent. Irene served as Chair and Priscilla served as Secretary.

 

Irene called the meeting to order at 6:40 pm and blew out the candles on the birthday cake that Kimberly had made for Irene’s and Carrie’s birthday.

 

The minutes of the November 29, 2004, meeting were read and approved.

 

Financial Report

 

            The Financial Report was mailed to Carrie who was absent, so there was no report for this meeting.

 

Old Business

 

Super Party post mortem:  All agreed that the party was a great success. Several Committee members reported compliments and raves from attendees about the entertainment and the caterer, with many people saying it was the best party yet. Several changes were suggested for next year:  move the dessert table so that people didn’t eat dessert before or instead of dinner (tsk). Paul suggested that we keep the program short next year.

 

New Business

 

 Irene and Kimberly reported that Jim Downey had died unexpectedly very recently and suggested that Mike put a commemoration on the Spearhead website that would include a request for donations to be made to the Supers rather than flowers for the memorial.

 Paul brought up the possibility of another garage sale.  Charlie Lichtman was willing to host it again and April 30 was proposed as the date, which would be posted on the website.  Paul would discuss with Charlie the particulars of when he will accept donations and what sorts of items are acceptable.

 

            Then followed discussion of who is responsible for having the Super of the Year plaque engraved.

 

Bake sale dates for the summer season were discussed:  during Pearlfishers (June 26 and July 3), Queen of Spades (June 12, June 19), Cosí fan tutte (July 10—last day of season).  Susan suggested providing curbside pickup of food from donors to make it easier for them to bring items for the bake sale.

 

Mike suggested discontinuing the hard copy of the newsletter. Susan suggested that we phase it out gradually and send a bulleted list of news items and agenda items.

 

Susan said she would let us know when she is going to look at the San Francisco Yacht Club as a possible (free!) venue for this year’s party.

 

A general discussion of the new season followed.

 

The meeting was adjourned at 7:25.

 

The next meeting will be March 21, 2005, at 6:30 pm in the 4th floor conference room of the Opera House. Kimberly will act as chair.
