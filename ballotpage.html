ELECTION OF THE SAN FRANCISCO OPERA SUPER COMMITTEE
July 2004 – June 2005



The composition of the Committee is comprised of four elected members, four members appointed by the House from the remaining candidates and the current Super Coordinator.

Committee members are expected to attend all of the monthly meetings, participate in all fund-raising activities such as Bake and Garage Sales, help to put on the Annual Party in December, and act as liaison between Supers and the Committee or House.

In the past few seasons the House has relied more heavily on the participation of Production Super Captains who are expected to communicate with Supers in that production, notify them of the schedule and oversee the sign in process. Not all Production Super Captains are drawn from the Committee but Committee Members should be willing to take on this responsibility.

The Term of Office is one year beginning in July of 2004 and ending in June of 2005. Committee members can only serve three consecutive terms, after which they must wait one year before again being eligible to run.

The members of the new 2004 - 2005 Committee are: Irene Bechtel, Michael Harvey, Priscilla Lore, Susan Anderson-Norby, Paul Szcsesiul, Kimberly Thompson, Sally Warren, and Rob Wonder. The 2003 committee welcomed new members at the regular meeting on July 12, below.
