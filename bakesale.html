Baked Goods News


Our Il trovatore bake sale on June 15th netted about $525 for the Super Coffers.

Set up was by Larry Severino and returning favorites included Charlie Lichtman's Bedevilled Eggs and Traude Alpert's Variations on an Apple Cake.

Our new Super Coordinator (not Captain, please) Carrie Murphy brought Brownies, that were gone within seconds, and Men's Dresser Joe Potter contributed an exotic Ceci Salad. Bjon Farber's daughter Alexandra baked some excellent chocolate chip cookies in lieu of a Father's Day gift.

Many thanks to all who contributed, served, bought and donated. Proceeds will, of course, go towards our Annual Super Party.
